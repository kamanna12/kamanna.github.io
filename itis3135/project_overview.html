Informative Website Development Plan

1. Project Overview:

Application Description and Purpose: The website aims to provide comprehensive information about lung cancer screening, including its importance, the target audience, and available resources. It serves as a platform to raise awareness about the significance of timely screenings and offers guidance to potential users.

Intended Users: The website targets individuals who may be at risk for lung cancer, caregivers, healthcare professionals, and anyone seeking information about lung cancer screening.

Overview of Website Content: The website will cover topics such as the importance of lung cancer screening, risk factors, screening guidelines, available screening methods, frequently asked questions, resources for further information, and support networks.

6. Dynamic Functionality:

The purpose of incorporating dynamic functionality is to streamline user interactions, making the website more intuitive and efficient to navigate. For instance, interactive tools like the Risk Assessment Tool empower users to evaluate their risk factors for lung cancer conveniently, fostering greater awareness and understanding of health-related issues. Similarly, the Cost Calculator on the FAQ's page enables users to estimate screening costs swiftly, enhancing accessibility to important information. Overall, dynamic functionality enriches the website's usability, enabling users to interact more effectively with its content and features, thereby improving their overall experience.

Examples include:

  • Interactive Risk Assessment Tool: This will be implemented on the Screening Guidelines page to help users assess their risk factors for lung cancer. Similar functionality to the forms can be seen on the forms tab of my main website.
